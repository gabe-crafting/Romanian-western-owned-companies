# IsoSkills Oy

## Identity
- **Country:** Finland
- **Type:** Oy (private limited company; IT services)
- **Role:** Parent of IsoSkills Group SRL; Finnish nearshore / software development group with delivery centers in Finland and Romania

## What it does
- Finnish IT services company (Oy) founded 2016.
- Nearshore software development for banking, payments and enterprise clients.
- Parent of IsoSkills Group SRL Bucharest delivery centre.

## Short history
- Founded in 2016 by IT veterans; operates delivery centers in Finland and Romania combining Nordic reliability with nearshore expertise ([IsoSkills](https://isoskills.fi/about-us/)).
- BRCC member profile describes IsoSkills Group SRL as the Bucharest delivery center of the Finnish parent ([BRCC](https://brcconline.eu/member/isoskills-group/)).
- Holds ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and ISO 27001 certifications; serves banking, payments and enterprise software clients ([IsoSkills](https://isoskills.fi/about-us/)).
- IsoSkills Group SRL reported 12 employees and ~45.1M RON turnover in 2025; Romanian registry does not publicly expose shareholder names ([Firme Online](https://www.firme-on-line.ro/profile/38292530.html)).
