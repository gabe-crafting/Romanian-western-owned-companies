# Hill International (UK) Ltd

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Type:** Private limited company (construction programme / project management)
- **Role:** UK parent of Hill International (Bucharest) SRL

## What it does
- UK-registered entity within the global Hill International construction and infrastructure consultancy group.
- Provides programme, project and construction management services through an international office network.
- Parent of Romanian subsidiary Hill International (Bucharest) SRL per group SEC disclosures.

## Short history
- Hill International (UK) Ltd is registered at Companies House (03561760) with registered office in Exeter, Devon ([Companies House](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/03561760)).
