# Mott MacDonald Group

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Type:** Employee-owned global consultancy (private)
- **Role:** UK parent of Mott MacDonald Romania SRL

## What it does
- Global engineering, management and development consultancy across infrastructure, transport, energy, water and buildings.
- One of the largest employee-owned consultancies worldwide (~19,500 staff).
- Provides programme management, design, advisory and sustainability services in 150+ countries.

## Online presence
- **Website:** https://www.mottmac.com/
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/company/mott-macdonald

## Short history
- Formed in 1989 from merger of UK consultancies Mott, Hay and Anderson and Sir M MacDonald & Partners ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mott_MacDonald)).
- Headquartered in London/Croydon, UK; employee trust ownership model since 1995 ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mott_MacDonald)).
- Mott MacDonald Romania SRL established 1998 in Bucharest with UK foreign capital ([FirmeOnLine](https://www.firme-on-line.ro/profile/11071473.html)).
