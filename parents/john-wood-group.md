# John Wood Group / Wood

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom (Scotland / Aberdeen)
- **Type:** Global engineering and consulting group (formerly listed, now private)
- **Role:** UK-headquartered parent of Wood Operations SRL (formerly Amec Operations)

## What it does
- Global engineering, project delivery and technical consultancy for energy, oil & gas, renewables and industrial markets.
- Operates in 60+ countries following 2017 merger with Amec Foster Wheeler.
- Provides project management, design, and operations support across the energy transition.

## Short history
- John Wood Group P.l.c. merged with Amec Foster Wheeler in October 2017 ([Wood](https://www.woodgroup.com/news/wood-group-completes-acquisition-of-amec-foster-wheeler)).
- LEI registry lists AMEC OPERATIONS SRL as a consolidated subsidiary ([LEI.report](https://lei.report/LEI/549300PLYY6I10B6S323)).
- Wood Romania has operated since 2012 per BRCC member profile ([BRCC](https://brcconline.eu/member/amec-operations-srl/)).
- Wood was delisted from the London Stock Exchange in March 2026 following acquisition by Sidara ([Wood investor relations](https://www.woodgroup.com/investors/investor-relations-contacts)).
