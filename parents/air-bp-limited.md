# Air BP Limited / bp

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Type:** Private limited company (aviation fuels, bp group)
- **Role:** UK aviation fuels entity within bp plc group

## What it does
- Wholesale of aviation fuels and related products (SIC 46719).
- Part of bp's global Air bp aviation business serving airports and airlines.
- UK-registered component of bp group structure owning/operating aviation fuel subsidiaries.

## Online presence
- **Website:** https://www.bp.com/en_global/bp-air.html

## Short history
- Air BP Limited is registered at Companies House (01150609), incorporated 1973, active ([Companies House](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/01150609)).
- bp group aviation operations in Romania run through Air BP Sales Romania SRL and Romanian Fuelling Services SRL at Otopeni ([BoardingPass](https://boardingpass.ro/tarom-vinde-participatia-detinuta-in-air-bp-romania/)).
- TAROM sold its 50% stake in Romanian Fuelling Services to BP Holdings BV, making bp sole owner ([BoardingPass](https://boardingpass.ro/tarom-vinde-participatia-detinuta-in-air-bp-romania/)).
