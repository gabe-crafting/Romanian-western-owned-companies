# Qodea Technology Limited

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Type:** Private limited company (Google Cloud systems integrator)
- **Role:** UK parent of Appsbroker SRL (Iași); formerly Appsbroker Limited

## What it does
- Google Cloud systems integration — data, AI, security and infrastructure consulting.
- Europe’s largest dedicated Google Cloud partner following 2023 merger of Appsbroker and Cloud Technology Solutions (CTS).
- Operates across UK, Benelux and wider Europe; Romanian delivery via Appsbroker SRL in Iași.

## Online presence
- **Website:** https://www.bynd.com/eu/
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/company/qodea

## Short history
- Originally incorporated February 2006 as Ready2GoIT Limited; renamed Appsbroker Consulting Limited (2010), Appsbroker Limited (2018), Qodea Technology Limited (2024) ([Companies House](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/05702796)).
- Founded by Mike Conner in Swindon; grew to Google’s largest Premier Partner in EMEA ([Qodea](https://www.bynd.com/eu/about-us)).
- Merged with CTS in 2023; rebranded Qodea in 2024 ([ChannelLife](https://channellife.co.uk/story/appsbroker-rebrands-as-qodea-following-successful-merger)).
