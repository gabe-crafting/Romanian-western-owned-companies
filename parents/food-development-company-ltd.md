# Food Development Company Ltd

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom (Scottish-led)
- **Type:** Private limited company (impact investment / food enterprise development)
- **Role:** Parent of Transylvania Food Company SRL (Pivnița Bunicii)

## What it does
- UK private company promoting socially responsible impact investment in food enterprises.
- Parent of Transylvania Food Company SRL (Pivnița Bunicii) in Saschiz, Mureș.
- Scottish-led; founded 2009 by Jim Turnbull to develop sustainable food businesses.

## Online presence
- **Website:** https://www.fooddevco.com/

## Short history
- Scottish entrepreneur Jim Turnbull is CEO of Food Development Company Ltd and director of Transylvania Food Company SRL ([Business Review](https://business-review.eu/sidebar-featured/jim-turnbull-zacusca-253129)).
- Transylvania Food Company SRL registered in Romania in 2010 and operates the Pivnița Bunicii brand from Saschiz, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Mureș ([Business Review](https://business-review.eu/sidebar-featured/jim-turnbull-zacusca-253129)).
- Company materials and interviews describe TFC as a wholly owned subsidiary of the UK-based Food Development Company Ltd ([Business Review](https://business-review.eu/sidebar-featured/jim-turnbull-zacusca-253129)).
- TFC reported 11 employees in 2024, producing traditional jams, zacusca and preserves for domestic and export markets ([Termene](https://termene.ro/firma/26381774-TRANSYLVANIA-FOOD-COMPANY-SRL)).
