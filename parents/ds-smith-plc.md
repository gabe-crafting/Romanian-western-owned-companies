# DS Smith plc

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom (London headquarters; EMEA packaging group)
- **Type:** Public packaging group (formerly LSE: SMDS)
- **Role:** Parent group for DS Smith’s Romanian paper, packaging and recycling operations

## What it does
- Global fibre-based packaging, paper-making and recycling business.
- Corrugated packaging for FMCG, e-commerce and industrial customers.
- Operates Romania’s largest packaging-paper mill (Zărnești) plus box plants in Ghimbav and Timișoara ([DS Smith](https://www.dssmith.com/)).

## Short history
- Founded in 1940; long listed on the London Stock Exchange as a FTSE 100 constituent ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DS_Smith), [BRCC](https://brcconline.eu/member/ds-smith/)).
- Acquired the Zărnești paper mill (then Ecopaper) in 2018 ([DS Smith](https://www.dssmith.com/company/locations/our-paper-mills/zarnesti-paper-mill)).
- Acquired by US International Paper in February 2025; EMEA operations continue under the DS Smith brand pending a planned spin-off ([Yahoo Finance UK](https://uk.finance.yahoo.com/news/ds-smith-set-london-market-151759346.html)).
- BRCC member profile lists Romanian entities including Paper Zărnești, packaging plants in Ghimbav/Timișoara and recycling in Otopeni/Cluj ([BRCC](https://brcconline.eu/member/ds-smith/)).
