# Wirtek A/S

## Identity
- **Country:** Denmark
- **Type:** Public company (Nasdaq First North Copenhagen, ticker WIRTEK)
- **Role:** Parent of Wirtek SRL; IT engineering group headquartered in Aalborg

## What it does
- Danish IT engineering group (A/S), listed on Nasdaq First North Copenhagen.
- Software development, energy and IoT solutions across Denmark, Portugal and Romania.
- Parent of Wirtek SRL Cluj-Napoca; founded 2001 as Nokia spin-off.

## Short history
- Founded in 2001 in Aalborg as a spin-off from Nokia; listed on Nasdaq First North Copenhagen since 2006 ([Wirtek](https://www.wirtek.com/about-us)).
- Opened its first Romania tech hub in Cluj-Napoca and later expanded to Bucharest; group reports 200+ engineers across Denmark, Portugal and Romania ([Wirtek](https://www.wirtek.com/about-us)).
- Recent acquisitions include Software Pro A/S (2018), CoreBuild (2021), Seluxit and Pragmasoft (2024), expanding energy, IoT and software capabilities ([Wirtek](https://www.wirtek.com/about-us)).
- Wirtek SRL in Cluj-Napoca is identified as part of the Danish-listed Wirtek group; 63 employees and ~35.5M RON turnover in 2025 ([Firme Online](https://www.firme-on-line.ro/profile/18788530.html), [eFin](https://firme.efin.ro/wirtek-srl-cui-18788530)).
