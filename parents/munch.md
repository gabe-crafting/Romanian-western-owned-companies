# Munch

## Identity
- **Country:** Hungary
- **Type:** Private group (anti-food-waste mobile app platform)
- **Role:** Group controller after 2025 merger with Romanian Bonapp.eco; operates locally through Bonapp.eco SRL

## What it does
- Hungarian anti-food-waste mobile app platform operating in Hungary, Czechia, Slovakia and Romania.
- Connects partner restaurants and retailers with consumers to sell surplus food.
- Acquired Romanian Bonapp.eco in 2025; operates locally through Bonapp.eco SRL.

## Short history
- Hungarian anti-food-waste app leader active in Hungary, Czechia and Slovakia; entered Romania in March 2024 ([Ziarul Financiar](https://www.zf.ro/business-hi-tech/start-up-ul-romanesc-bonapp-eco-intra-grupul-ungar-munch-prezent-22689835)).
- 2025 merger integrated Bonapp.eco into the Munch group; the two apps are to become a single platform based on Munch's technology ([Ziarul Financiar](https://www.zf.ro/business-hi-tech/start-up-ul-romanesc-bonapp-eco-intra-grupul-ungar-munch-prezent-22689835)).
- Combined group reported 7.5 million EUR revenue in the prior year with 6,000+ partner locations across four countries ([Ziarul Financiar](https://www.zf.ro/business-hi-tech/start-up-ul-romanesc-bonapp-eco-intra-grupul-ungar-munch-prezent-22689835)).
- Bonapp.eco founders and investors remain in the business; Munch operates in Romania through Bonapp.eco SRL, but post-merger shareholding was not disclosed ([Ziarul Financiar](https://www.zf.ro/business-hi-tech/start-up-ul-romanesc-bonapp-eco-intra-grupul-ungar-munch-prezent-22689835)).
