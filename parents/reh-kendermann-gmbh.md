# Reh Kendermann GmbH

## Identity
- **Country:** Germany
- **Type:** GmbH (wine group)
- **Role:** Parent of Carl Reh Winery SRL (Crama Oprișor); headquartered in Bingen am Rhein

## What it does
- German wine group (GmbH) headquartered in Bingen am Rhein.
- International wine production, export and private-label services; owns Crama Oprișor in Romania.
- Founded Carl Reh Winery SRL in 1994; among Germany's largest wine exporters.

## Online presence
- **Website:** https://www.reh-kendermann.de/
- **Facebook:** https://www.facebook.com/RehKendermann

## Short history
- Founded Carl Reh Winery SRL in 1994; the Romanian winery remains part of the Reh Kendermann group ([Comunicate de Presă](https://www.comunicatedepresa.ro/sc-carl-reh-winery-srl/)).
- Described in company communications as one of Germany's most modern wineries, the leading exporter of German wines and the largest wine importer in Germany ([Comunicate de Presă](https://www.comunicatedepresa.ro/sc-carl-reh-winery-srl/)).
- Carl Reh Winery operates Crama Oprișor in Mehedinți and is among Romania's significant wine exporters under DO Romania ([Comunicate de Presă](https://www.comunicatedepresa.ro/sc-carl-reh-winery-srl/)).
- Romanian entity reported 98 employees and ~25.8M RON turnover in 2025 ([Risco](https://www.risco.ro/en/verifica-firma/carl-reh-winery-cui-6121230)).
