# APERIO INTELLIGENCE LIMITED

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Type:** Private limited company (corporate intelligence and financial crime advisory)
- **Role:** Parent of Aperio Intelligence Romania SRL; London-based firm with offices in the UK, France and Romania

## What it does
- London-based corporate intelligence and financial-crime advisory firm.
- Enhanced due diligence, investigations and market-risk analysis for financial institutions.
- Parent of Aperio Intelligence Romania SRL; founded 2014 by Adrian Ford.

## Short history
- Founded in 2014 by Adrian Ford, previously a KPMG LLP London forensic director who led corporate intelligence services for over 10 years ([BRCC](https://brcconline.eu/member/aperio-intelligence-romania-srl/)).
- Offers enhanced due diligence, legal advisory and investigatory services, and country or market risk analysis to financial institutions and asset managers ([BRCC](https://brcconline.eu/member/aperio-intelligence-romania-srl/)).
- UK Companies House PSC filing shows British national Adrian Ford with 75%+ shares in the parent ([Companies House](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/09164101/persons-with-significant-control)).
- Operates a Cluj-Napoca office through Aperio Intelligence Romania SRL ([BRCC](https://brcconline.eu/member/aperio-intelligence-romania-srl/)).
