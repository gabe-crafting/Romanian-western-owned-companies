# Layershift Limited

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Type:** Private limited company (managed cloud hosting)
- **Role:** Parent of Layershift Technologies SRL (Cluj-Napoca)

## What it does
- Managed cloud hosting, VPS, dedicated servers and PaaS solutions.
- UK-headquartered hosting provider operating since 2001 with infrastructure across Europe, North America and Asia.
- Romanian subsidiary provides IT consultancy and technical delivery from Cluj-Napoca.
