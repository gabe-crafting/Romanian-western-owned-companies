# amb Holding

## Identity
- **Country:** Austria
- **Type:** Private holding group
- **Role:** Parent of Romanian wine producer AMB Wine Company SRL (Crama Liliac) and related agribusiness / real-estate interests in Romania

## What it does
- Austrian private holding group owned by Alfred Michael Beck.
- Wine (amb Wine / Crama Liliac), agribusiness, real estate and forestry interests in Romania.
- Parent of AMB Wine Company SRL; vineyards in Lechința and Batoș, Transylvania.

## Online presence
- **Website:** https://liliac.com/

## Short history
- Austrian businessman Alfred Michael Beck owns amb Holding, which includes amb Wine Company, amb Agro Company, amb Immobilien and amb Silva Property ([Business Review](https://business-review.eu/featured/amb-wine-launches-new-wine-brand-plans-to-double-sales-in-2014-59454)).
- amb Agro Company was the first division in the group, established in 2006; amb Wine operates vineyards in Transylvania (Lechința and Batoș areas) ([Business Review](https://business-review.eu/featured/amb-wine-launches-new-wine-brand-plans-to-double-sales-in-2014-59454)).
- 2014 press reported AMB Wine launching the Crepuscul brand and planning to double sales; Victor Ciuperca was named country manager of amb Holding ([Business Review](https://business-review.eu/featured/amb-wine-launches-new-wine-brand-plans-to-double-sales-in-2014-59454)).
- Romanian registry lists Austrian foreign private capital for AMB Wine Company SRL ([ListaFirme](https://listafirme.ro/amb-wine-company-srl-17995031/)).
