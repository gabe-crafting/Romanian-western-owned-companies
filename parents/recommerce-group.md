# Recommerce Group

## Identity
- **Country:** France
- **Type:** Private group (refurbished high-tech products, especially smartphones)
- **Role:** Parent of Recommerce Devices and Solutions RO SRL (former Fenix.eco)

## What it does
- French private group — European pioneer in refurbishment of high-end tech products.
- Refurbished smartphones and electronics; active across Western and Eastern Europe.
- Parent of Recommerce Devices and Solutions RO SRL (former Fenix.eco) in Bucharest.

## Short history
- European pioneer in refurbishment of high-end products; completed its first acquisitions in 2021, including Romanian startup Fenix.eco and French testing app Deal Certify ([Romania Insider](https://www.romania-insider.com/french-recommerce-fenix-eco-acquisition)).
- Already active in Switzerland, Belgium, the Netherlands and Germany; aimed to cover all European countries by 2022 ([Romania Insider](https://www.romania-insider.com/french-recommerce-fenix-eco-acquisition)).
- Group recorded over 40% revenue growth to EUR 90 million in the year before the Fenix.eco deal; international sales accounted for 50% of volume ([Romania Insider](https://www.romania-insider.com/french-recommerce-fenix-eco-acquisition)).
- Fenix.eco renamed Recommerce Devices & Solutions RO SRL from 1 July 2021; group targeted EUR 10 million Eastern Europe revenue within three years ([Romania Insider](https://www.romania-insider.com/french-recommerce-fenix-eco-acquisition)).
