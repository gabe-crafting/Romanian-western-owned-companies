# Teconnex Ltd

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Type:** Private limited company (global jointing / fabricated metal products group)
- **Role:** Parent of Teconnex Europe S.R.L.; part of Hexadex Group, headquartered in Keighley, West Yorkshire

## What it does
- UK-founded global jointing and fabricated metal products group (Hexadex Group).
- Emissions and industrial jointing solutions; factories on four continents.
- Parent of Teconnex Europe S.R.L. in Tureni, Cluj — group's largest production site.

## Online presence
- **Website:** https://teconnex.com/
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/company/teconnex-uk/

## Short history
- UK-founded Teconnex Group supplies jointing solutions for emissions and diversified industrial sectors, with factories on four continents ([Transilvania Business](https://www.transilvaniabusiness.ro/2023/07/31/in-cel-mult-trei-ani-fabrica-teconnex-de-la-tureni-va-deveni-cea-mai-mare-locatie-a-grupului/)).
- Teconnex Ltd is registered at Bronte Works, Chesham Street, Keighley, West Yorkshire; Teconnex Europe S.R.L. operates in Tureni, Cluj County ([Teconnex](https://teconnex.com/where-we-are/)).
- 2023 press reported the Tureni factory would become the group's largest site within three years ([Transilvania Business](https://www.transilvaniabusiness.ro/2023/07/31/in-cel-mult-trei-ani-fabrica-teconnex-de-la-tureni-va-deveni-cea-mai-mare-locatie-a-grupului/)).
- Romanian subsidiary Teconnex Europe S.R.L. reported 202 employees and ~103.7M RON turnover in 2024 ([AtlasFirme](https://www.atlasfirme.com/firme/teconnex-europe-s-r-l-39332757)).
