# Dunlop Mills Ltd

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Type:** Private limited company (chartered accountants and registered auditors)
- **Role:** Parent of Dunlop Mills SRL; UK firm with a Bucharest subsidiary since 1999

## What it does
- UK chartered accountancy firm and registered auditor.
- Accounting, auditing and tax services for 250+ UK clients.
- Parent of Dunlop Mills SRL, Bucharest subsidiary since 1999.

## Online presence
- **Website:** https://www.dunlopmills.ro/

## Short history
- Chartered accountancy firm with a client portfolio of more than 250 in the UK ([Dunlop Mills Romania](https://www.dunlopmills.ro/e_general.html)).
- Extended international accounting support services to Romania in 1999 through Dunlop Mills SRL ([Dunlop Mills Romania](https://www.dunlopmills.ro/e_general.html)).
- Dunlop Mills SRL is a member of the British-Romanian Chamber of Commerce and provides accounting, auditing and tax consultancy in Bucharest ([Dunlop Mills Romania](https://www.dunlopmills.ro/e_general.html), [BRCC](https://brcc.bg/en/members/m/dunlop-mills-srl)).
- Romanian subsidiary reported 7 employees and ~2.6M RON turnover in 2025 ([Firme Online](https://www.firme-on-line.ro/profile/12271909.html)).
