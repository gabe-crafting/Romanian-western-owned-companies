# Intertek Group plc

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Type:** Public limited company (LSE: ITRK)
- **Role:** Ultimate parent of Intertek Industry Services Romania SRL

## What it does
- Global assurance, testing, inspection and certification (ATIC) services.
- Laboratories and offices in 100+ countries serving consumer products, energy, industry and infrastructure clients.
- Parent of the named Romanian testing subsidiary.

## Online presence
- **Website:** https://www.intertek.com/
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/company/intertek

## Short history
- Over 130 years of history; incorporated in England & Wales with London headquarters at 33 Cavendish Square ([Intertek annual report](https://www.intertek.com/siteassets/investors/2026/ara/intertek-financial-report-2025.pdf)).
- FTSE 100 listed group with ~45,000 employees globally (2025 annual report).
- Romanian entity Intertek Industry Services Romania SRL provides technical testing and analysis (CAEN 7120) from Bucharest ([FirmeOnLine](https://www.firme-on-line.ro/profile/12841309.html)).
