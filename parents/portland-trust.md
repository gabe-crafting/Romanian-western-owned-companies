# Portland Trust

## Identity
- **Country:** Czech Republic (Czech-founded; Western developer)
- **Type:** Private real-estate development and property management group
- **Role:** Parent of Portland Management SRL; commercial developer with offices in Prague and Bucharest

## What it does
- Czech-founded commercial real-estate developer and property manager.
- Develops and manages offices, retail and logistics assets in the Czech Republic and Romania.
- Parent of Portland Management SRL; long-standing partnership with Ares Management.

## Online presence
- **Website:** https://www.portlandtrust.ro/
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/company/portlandtrust

## Short history
- Founded in 1997; focuses on commercial property — offices, retail and logistics — in prime or developing infrastructure locations ([Portland Trust](https://www.portlandtrust.ro/ro/despre-noi)).
- Has developed ~800,000 m² of commercial space and manages ~600,000 m² of assets across the Czech Republic and Romania ([Portland Trust](https://www.portlandtrust.ro/ro/despre-noi)).
- Long-standing partnership with Ares Management Corporation (US investment fund) enabled large urban projects across Central and Eastern Europe ([Portland Trust](https://www.portlandtrust.ro/ro/despre-noi)).
- Portland Management SRL provides property management in Bucharest; 9 employees and ~3.8M RON turnover in 2025 ([Firme Online](https://www.firme-on-line.ro/profile/13488869.html)).
