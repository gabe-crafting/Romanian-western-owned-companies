# M247 Group

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom (founded); global operations including Romania
- **Type:** Private group (hosting, data centres, network infrastructure)
- **Role:** Parent network for M247 Europe SRL (Bucharest legal entity / M247 Global brand)

## What it does
- UK-founded hosting, colocation, cloud and connectivity provider tracing roots to Manchester (~2000).
- Operates carrier-neutral Tier III data centres and a multi-continent network (AS9009).
- Bucharest (M247 Europe SRL) hosts group HQ functions and European core infrastructure for M247 Global.

## Online presence
- **Website:** https://m247global.com/
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/company/m247-global

## Short history
- M247 was born in Manchester, England, and expanded into Romania through subsidiary M247 Europe SRL from 2012–2013 ([Data Center Dynamics](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/british-firm-opens-its-first-data-center-in-romania/), [Wall-Street.ro](https://www.wall-street.ro/articol/IT-C-Tehnologie/162091/p-m247-ltd-una-dintre-cele-mai-importante-companii-itc-din-europa-intra-pe-piata-din-romania.html)).
- M247 Europe built the group's first non-UK data centre in Bucharest; the Romanian operation later became the base for M247 Global ([Data Center Dynamics](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/british-firm-opens-its-first-data-center-in-romania/), [M247 Global](https://www.m247global.com/romania-dedicated-server)).
- In June 2025 the group completed sale of UK-only connectivity operations to Convergence Group while retaining international operations through M247 Global in Bucharest ([Business Review](https://business-review.eu/tech/m247-group-completes-sale-of-uk-only-connectivity-and-it-services-operations-to-convergence-group-284891)).
- M247 Europe SRL reported 72 employees and ~26.2M RON turnover in 2025 ([ListaFirme](https://listafirme.eu/m247-europe-srl-30203422/)).
