# OBERALP SPA

## Identity
- **Country:** Italy
- **Type:** SPA (private group)
- **Role:** Owner of Castel Vinum SRL (Villa Vinèa winery); broader Oberalp group led by Heiner Oberrauch

## What it does
- Italian private group led by founder Heiner Oberrauch (Bolzano).
- Owns Castel Vinum SRL and Villa Vinèa winery in Transylvania's Târnave region.
- Wine investment and production across ~70 ha; broader Oberalp group interests.

## Short history
- Wine-industry directory lists OBERALP SPA as owner of Castel Vinum SRL / Villa Vinèa ([Crama România](https://www.crameromania.ro/en/wineries/villa-vinea-castel-vinum-203.html)).
- Heiner Oberrauch, born in Bolzano, Italy, is identified as founder and owner of the Oberalp group ([Villa Vinèa](https://villavinea.com/en/2019/01/24/heiner-oberrauch-founder-and-owner/)).
- Villa Vinèa winery investment began in 2006 with inauguration in 2012; ~70 ha in the Târnave / Dealurile Transilvaniei area ([Crama România](https://www.crameromania.ro/en/wineries/villa-vinea-castel-vinum-203.html)).
- Castel Vinum SRL reported 16 employees and ~3.5M RON turnover in 2025; registry shows foreign private capital ([Firme Online](https://www.firme-on-line.ro/profile/16429263.html)).
