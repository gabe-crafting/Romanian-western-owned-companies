# Mec Com Limited

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Companies House:** 03954360
- **Type:** Private limited company (contract manufacturer)
- **Role:** UK parent of European Fabrications SRL (Cluj-Napoca)

## What it does
- UK independent contract manufacturer specialising in sheet-metal fabrications, electromechanical assembly and testing.
- Serves power distribution, healthcare, industrial process control and related sectors.
- Operates UK facilities in Staffordshire and a Romanian manufacturing site via European Fabrications.

## Online presence
- **Website:** https://mec-com.co.uk/
- **LinkedIn:** https://uk.linkedin.com/company/mec-com

## Short history
- Incorporated in March 2000; registered office at Hixon Airfield Industrial Estate, Stafford ([Companies House](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/03954360)).
- Company site and LinkedIn describe European Fabrications in Cluj-Napoca as the low-cost manufacturing arm ([Mec Com](https://mec-com.co.uk/), [European Fabrications](https://www.europeanfabrications.ro/)).
- Trade press profiles Mec Com as one of the UK’s largest independent contract manufacturers with dual UK and Romania manufacturing ([Manufacturing Today](https://manufacturing-today.com/news/mec-com-ltd/)).
