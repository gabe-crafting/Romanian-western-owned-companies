# Gleeds International Limited

## Identity
- **Country:** United Kingdom
- **Type:** Private limited company (global construction consultancy)
- **Role:** Parent network for Gleeds Romania SRL and related Romanian entities

## What it does
- UK-headquartered global construction and property consultancy, independent since 1885.
- Project management, cost consultancy, construction management and engineering design.
- Parent network for Gleeds Romania SRL and related Romanian entities.

## Short history
- UK-headquartered independent construction consultancy with a Romania presence since 1998 ([Gleeds](https://www.gleeds.com/en/locations/mainland-europe/romania/)).
- Gleeds Romania provides project management, construction management, quality monitoring and site inspector (dirigenție de șantier) services in Bucharest ([Gleeds](https://www.gleeds.com/en/locations/mainland-europe/romania/)).
- UK accounts for Gleeds International Limited list Gleeds Construction Management Services SRL at 52% and Gleeds Romania as part of the group network ([The Accounts](https://theaccounts.uk/company/06213613/gleeds-international-limited/)).
- Gleeds Romania SRL reported 5 employees in 2025 ([ListaFirme](https://listafirme.eu/gleeds-romania-srl-12846640/)).
