## Identity

- **CUI:** 14787842
- **Status:** Active
- **Location:** Timișoara, Timiș
- **Industry / CAEN:** 1721 — Manufacture of corrugated paper and paperboard and of containers of paper and paperboard

## What it does
- Corrugated packaging manufacture (CAEN 1721) at DS Smith’s Timișoara box plant on Calea Torontalului.
- Serves western Romania and Banat industrial and FMCG customers within DS Smith’s integrated packaging network.
- Sister plant to DS Smith Packaging Ghimbav; fed by Zărnești recycled paper production.
