## What it does
- Manufactures metal components and sub-assemblies for specialist electro-mechanical and electronic control systems (CAEN 2540).
- Low-cost manufacturing arm of UK contract manufacturer Mec Com Limited in Cluj-Napoca.
- Serves market leaders in power distribution, healthcare and industrial control sectors.
