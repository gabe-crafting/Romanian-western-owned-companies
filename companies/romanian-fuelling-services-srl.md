## What it does
- Warehousing and storage (CAEN 5210) of aviation fuel at Otopeni airport.
- Operates fuelling and storage infrastructure used by Air BP at Henri Coandă International Airport.
- Previously a TAROM–Air BP joint venture; now bp-controlled.

## Short history
- Romanian Fuelling Services SRL was incorporated in August 2003 at Str. Aurel Vlaicu 59, Otopeni ([ListaFirme](https://listafirme.ro/romanian-fuelling-services-srl-9268008/)).
- Historically a 50/50 joint venture between TAROM and Air BP; TAROM sold its stake to BP Holdings BV, part of the UK-founded bp group ([BoardingPass](https://boardingpass.ro/tarom-vinde-participatia-detinuta-in-air-bp-romania/)).
- Reported 10 employees and ~10.4M RON turnover in 2024 ([Risco](https://www.risco.ro/verifica-firma/romanian-fuelling-services-cui-9268008)).
