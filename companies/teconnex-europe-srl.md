## What it does
- Manufacture of fabricated metal products (CAEN 2599) — jointing solutions at Tureni, Cluj.
- Emissions and industrial jointing components for automotive and diversified sectors.
- Romanian factory of UK-based Teconnex Group (Hexadex Group); group's largest production site.
