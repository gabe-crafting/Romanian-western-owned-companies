## What it does
- Hosting, colocation, cloud connectivity and network infrastructure (CAEN 6209) — Bucharest HQ of the M247 group.
- Operates Tier III carrier-neutral data centres (DC1 & DC2) and global network services (AS9009).
- Serves enterprise clients with dedicated servers, IP transit, IXP peering and disaster-recovery solutions across Europe and beyond.

## Short history
- M247 Europe SRL is described as the local subsidiary of UK-based M247 Ltd, entering the Romanian market in 2012 ([Wall-Street.ro](https://www.wall-street.ro/articol/IT-C-Tehnologie/162091/p-m247-ltd-una-dintre-cele-mai-importante-companii-itc-din-europa-intra-pe-piata-din-romania.html)).
- M247 was founded in Manchester, England; its Bucharest subsidiary built the group's first data centre outside the UK from 2013 ([Data Center Dynamics](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/british-firm-opens-its-first-data-center-in-romania/)).
- 2025 press reported M247 Global (legal entity M247 Europe SRL) continues as the group's operational base in Bucharest after divesting UK-only connectivity operations ([Business Review](https://business-review.eu/tech/m247-group-completes-sale-of-uk-only-connectivity-and-it-services-operations-to-convergence-group-284891)).
