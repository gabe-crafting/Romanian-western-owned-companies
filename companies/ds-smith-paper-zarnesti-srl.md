## What it does
- Manufactures packaging paper from recycled fibre at Romania’s largest paper mill of its kind (CAEN 1712).
- Supplies corrugated-packaging operations across DS Smith’s Romanian and regional network.
- Part of DS Smith’s Brașov county industrial cluster (paper mill plus related logistics sites).

## Short history
- Papermaking on the Zărnești site dates to 1852; mill renamed Ecopaper SA in 2002 before DS Smith acquisition in 2018 ([DS Smith](https://www.dssmith.com/company/locations/our-paper-mills/zarnesti-paper-mill)).
- DS Smith inaugurated a major wastewater-treatment investment in 2023, marking five years under group ownership ([DS Smith](https://www.dssmith.com/media/our-stories/2023/9/ds-smith-zarnesti-paper-mill-inaugurates-new-waste-water-treatment-plant-as-part-of-its-existing-11-million-euro-investment-plan)).
- BRCC member profile lists S.C. DS Smith Paper Zărnești as the UK group’s Romanian paper mill ([BRCC](https://brcconline.eu/member/ds-smith/)).
- DS Smith plc was UK-listed until acquisition by US International Paper in February 2025; EMEA operations continue under DS Smith branding ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DS_Smith)).
