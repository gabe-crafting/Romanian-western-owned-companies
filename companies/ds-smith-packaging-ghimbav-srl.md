## What it does
- Manufactures corrugated packaging and related paperboard products (CAEN 1721) at Ghimbav, Brașov county.
- Box plant serving DS Smith’s Romanian packaging network alongside the Zărnești paper mill and Timișoara plant.
- Part of DS Smith’s integrated paper-to-packaging supply chain in Transylvania.
